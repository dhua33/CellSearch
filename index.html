Cell Search

  • High Concept: Find a man's lost cell phone.
  • Player Experience Goals: Simple RPG elements such as NPC, world, and object interaction, all in a simple maze game.
  • Genre: Maze
  • Game play: Use the arrow keys or WASD to move and spacebar to talk to the NPC and pick up the quest item.
  • Prototype goal: Create a simple maze game using tilemaps and having the player have the ability to use different keys to move.
  • Features: Basic RPG movement and NPC and object interaction.
  • Setting/Premise: In a maze in some place.
  • Story: Someone lost their cell phone in a maze. He wants you to find it for him since he can't do it himself.
  • Target Audience: Everyone 10 and up who has interest in cell phones and mazes.
  • Play time: 1-5 mins.
  • Strategies: Talk to the NPC and track your movement.
